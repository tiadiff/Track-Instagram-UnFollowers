# 🕵️‍♂️ Instagram Unfollowers Scanner (Dark Minimal Edition)

A lightweight, zero-dependency JavaScript utility that securely analyzes your Instagram profile to identify users who don't follow you back.

> **Note:** This version has been completely redesigned with minimalism in mind. <br> No profile pictures, no checkboxes, no risky automation—just pure data presented in a sleek, console-like interface.

---

## ✨ Features

* **🌑 Terminal-Style Interface:** A clean, professional dark layout (`#0a0a0a`) inspired by developer environments to minimize eye strain.
* **⚡ Ultra-Lightweight:** Stripped of unnecessary profile picture fetching and useless badges. Scanning is now significantly faster and highly memory-efficient.
* **📜 Seamless Scrolling:** View your results in a single, continuous numbered list. Say goodbye to clunky, fragmented pagination.
* **🛡️ Security First:**
    * **No Login Required:** The script leverages your active browser session. Your credentials never leave your machine.
    * **No Auto-Unfollow:** All automation features (like mass unfollowing) have been entirely removed to protect your account from Instagram bans, action blocks, or restrictions.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

Zero installation required. The script runs entirely within your browser.

1. Navigate to [Instagram.com](https://www.instagram.com/) and log in to your account.
2. Open your browser's **Developer Tools**:
    * Press `F12` on your keyboard.
    * *Or* right-click anywhere on the page and select **Inspect**.
3. Navigate to the **Console** tab.
4. Copy the entire script from the `.js` file provided in this repository.
5. Paste the code into the Console and hit **Enter**.
6. A **SCAN** button will appear in the center of your screen. Click it to begin the analysis.

Unlike shady Chrome extensions or third-party apps:
